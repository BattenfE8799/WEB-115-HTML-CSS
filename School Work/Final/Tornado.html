Tornado:

A tornado is a rotating column of air extending from the base of a cloud down to the ground. Tornadoes are able to completely destroy structures, uproot trees, and hurl objects through the air like deadly missiles. It can do massive damage to structures, animals, and people.

Preparing for a Tornado

Before:

Have a plan.

What todo:

Have a secure windowless room, on the lowest floor, to go to. If you don't have one, find one nearby that you can use for emergencies.
An emergency shelter is great for this.

Be Ready:

Watch the weather. Get weather alerts and notifications.

What to do:

  • Get to a safety.
  • Knealing down and covering your head is the posture schools teach for tornados
